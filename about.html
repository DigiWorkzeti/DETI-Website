About Us

How Digiworkz was found...

In early 2019, our venture into the electronic security system product supply business in Batangas stemmed from our personal backgrounds.

Our team members complement each other's skills and expertise, enabling us to execute operations and deliver projects with a sense of pride and honor. We've faced challenges and seized opportunities resulting from unexpected events, which prompted us to be more proactive in providing services to those who require them beyond mere business necessities.

Our initial clients valued this approach, leading to an influx of new clients through their endorsements and referrals. Consequently, we relocated our operational base to Makati, Metro Manila, where the majority of our new clients have been coming from.

Currently, numerous prominent companies have confidence in our ability to meet their electronic security system needs. They trust that we will provide the highest quality and adhere to industry best practices. We are committed to maintaining this trust as we expand, ensuring that we outperform our competitors in terms of quality, service, and reliability.
